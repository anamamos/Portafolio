About

I design intuitive, scalable, user-centered products — and I care most about the point where business goals and human needs actually meet.

I lead design initiatives end to end, from research and design systems to high-fidelity execution, and work closely with cross-functional teams to ship work that moves real metrics. My decisions are research-driven and shaped by a habit of continuous improvement.

The goal is always the same: solutions that look considered and, more importantly, work — bridging what a business needs with an experience people genuinely want to use.
