Overview

A banking experience designed for frequency and clarity.

This project focused on designing a modular mobile banking experience that supports daily financial interactions while staying clear and easy to use.

The goal was a system that feels intuitive, flexible and visually engaging without compromising usability.

The challenge

Banking is used daily — but rarely designed for it.

People interact with financial apps constantly, often performing repetitive tasks under time pressure. A banking app has to respect that rhythm.

  • 01Simplify complex financial actions.
  • 02Reduce friction in high-frequency interactions.
  • 03Create a system that scales across multiple features.
Design choices, guided by product principles

A system that scales with the product.

01

Modular structure

The experience is divided into flexible sections — accounts, features, services — that can evolve over time.

02

Clear hierarchy of information

Balances, transactions and actions are prioritised visually so the important things are always obvious.

03

Low-friction interactions

Designed for repeated use with minimal cognitive effort.

Product thinking

Three layers, one coherent product.

01

Core banking experience

Managing accounts, balances and financial actions — the everyday essentials.

02

Engagement layer

Content and experiences that extend the product beyond transactions.

03

Service modules

Additional features that integrate everyday needs into the platform.

Visual system

Designed for clarity and depth.

The visual system combines depth, gradients and contrast to create a sense of structure while keeping financial data clear.

Components are built to scale across sections while preserving consistency and usability — a design system doing quiet, reliable work.

What research pointed to

Speed, consistency, flexibility.

  • 01Users prioritise speed.
  • 02Consistency reduces errors.
  • 03Flexibility improves engagement.
Reflection

Clarity and consistency, at high frequency.

This project reinforced the importance of designing for clarity and consistency in high-frequency financial interactions. The system supports fast navigation, modularity and a strong visual structure across features. Future iterations would improve personalisation, refine navigation patterns and optimise key flows based on real user behaviour.
